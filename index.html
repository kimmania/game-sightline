Your goal

Shade cells black so every numbered cell sees exactly its number of white cells in the four cardinal directions.

Black cells cannot touch orthogonally. All white cells must be orthogonally connected. Tap a cell to cycle: unknown → black → white. Long-press a numbered cell to see its sightlines.

Tip: numbered cells are always white. A red border on a numbered cell means its current visible count is impossible. A green border means it’s satisfied.
